Brewing Tips

Master methods for the best cup.

  1. Grind: Medium-fine. Use 16:1 water-to-coffee ratio.
  2. Rinse filter and pre-wet grounds to bloom for 30s.
  3. Pour slowly for 2.5–3 minutes total brew time.

  • Grind: Coarse. 12:1 water-to-coffee.
  • Steep 4 minutes, then press slowly and serve immediately.
  • Decant into a server to avoid over-extraction.

  • Fine grind, tamp evenly. Dose ~18g for a double.
  • Extraction time 25–30s targeting 36–40ml.
  • Adjust grind to control extraction.
